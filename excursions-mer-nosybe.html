Nosy Iranja

Nosy Iranja: a beautiful mysterious island
Those who want to visit Madagascar and especially the Nosy Be archipelago, would certainly have Nosy Iranja want to run up!
If you are looking for a paradise island where you can enjoy the sun and the sea, if you are looking for a secluded island in paradise origin you will find it here!
Book your trip to Madagascar now!
wo paradise islets interconnected To get to where you want the sun and the sea and at the same time enjoy a paradise island you will find it here!
Two small islands in the original state are connected to each other
As it is a remote island, you will have to be patient before reaching Nosy Iranja: about 1.5 hours by boat from the main island of Nosy Be, heading southwest.
The journey may seem long, but it's worth the wait!
In the middle of the ocean's infinity in a small speedboat, you may feel like regretting the trip, but once you're there, you'll wish you could stay there forever!
The mysterious island of Nosy Iranja will appear before your eyes like a mirage!
Two small islands covered with greenery connected by a strip of fine white coral sand about a kilometer long, a lagoon with transparent waters ranging from turquoise to dark green, a long magnificent beach ...
On the largest island (Nosy Iranja Be) there is a fishing village where you will feel like in paradise thanks to a seafood buffet. The smallest island (Nosy Iranja Kely) is privately owned and consists of a fishing village and a private bay dominated by a hotel.
Fantastic landscapes and activities
Inland, it's nice to stroll along the beach, swim and meet the villagers. When you but crosses the strip of land that connects the two islets, you have to beware of the tide that puts everything under water. Every picture you take is a postcard! You don't expect it, but the island even has a lighthouse!
In the small lush rainforest you can observe some lemurs that seem to occupy all of Madagascar, even in the most remote and remote corners. There are mango trees that offer delicious fruits. Whatever you choose to do or visit on this small piece of land in the middle of the ocean, it will be extraordinary! You will feel like you are stepping into a dream!
Nosy Iranja, the Turtle Island, is famous for its Marine fauna!
You will have the opportunity to see the turtles on Nosy Iranja Kely and observe various birds (terns, herons and sea eagles). You can also enjoy various nautical Amusements surrender!
Windsurfing, water skiing, jet skiing and of course snorkeling.
The water is transparent and you can clearly see parrotfish, tuna, barracuda, swordfish, manta rays, sardines, trevallies, mackerel, sharks and dolphins. Hundreds of humpback whales also come here between July and October.
Between October and December the Whale sharks the turn, which are completely harmless! What a miracle!

Nosy Komba

Nosy komba est la 2eme plus grand île de l'archipel de Nosy Be.
Plus de 4000 habitant peuplent cette île.
Nosy Komba est une île pleine de surprises et riches en activité à faire.
Cette île preservée regorge des plantes médicinales et des animaux sauvages comme : Lemurien(macaco),BOA et d'autres reptiles se font discrets elles sont tres proteger par la population dans cette île.
Un village nommée AMPANGORINA, le plus grand village de l'île et le plus vieux village de pecheur , aussi le lieu d'adoration des rois et reines SAKALAVA.

Nosy Tanikely

Le parc marin de Nosy tanikely, Nosy Tanikely, signifie : ‘’l’île de petit terre’’ se trouve milieu de la baie de Nosy Be a seulement 5 miles nautiques à l’ouest de Nosy Komba, cette petite île située dans la réserve naturelle sous-marine protégée et terrestre de tanikely. Il est strictement et très fortement conseille de se baigner dans cette aquarium grandeur nature ! vous y verrez des coraux de toutes sortes, des anémones une multitude de poissons de toutes les couleurs, des mérous, des tortues de mer, des requins léopard et bien d’autres surprises vous y attend tel que superbe et copieux déjeuner buffet traditionnel, le pied dans le sable.
